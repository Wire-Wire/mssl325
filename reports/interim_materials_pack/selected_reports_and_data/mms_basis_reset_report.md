## Root cause

MMS Phase 1 tetrahedron separation (~10 km) is ~100× smaller than the observed near-MP gradient spatial scale (~750–3750 km). Phase 2 separation (~25 km) improves this by only 2–3×, which is insufficient.

Gradient spatial scale must be within ~10× of tetrahedron separation. For MMS Phase 1 (~10 km): only ~10–100 km features. For Phase 2 (~25 km): only ~25–250 km features.
